# **App Name**: RoadWise 360

## Core Features:

- PESV Digitalization: Digitizes and automates the 24 steps of the Plan Estratégico de Seguridad Vial (PESV) as outlined by the Superintendencia de Transporte.
- Risk Assessment Assistant: AI-powered tool that evaluates risk based on provided data to determine a risk score and auto-assigns personnel to address these issues.
- Fleet and Asset Management: Manages vehicle lifecycles, maintenance schedules, repair records, and part traceability.
- Incident Reporting: Mobile app feature for reporting incidents with photos and geotagging for improved data and location accuracy.
- Automated Reporting: Generates SISI/PESV reports in the format required by the Superintendencia de Transporte, with e-signature capabilities.
- Real-time GPS Integration: Connects with GPS/OBD devices to track metrics like speed, harsh braking, and driving hours in real-time, generating custom notifications when any violation of preset company norms occurs.
- Driver Profiles: Centralizes driver data including licenses, medical exams, training, and expiration alerts.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep blue (#135BEC) to convey trust and security in road management. The system also needs a light theme (following designs based in figma), so the colors are versatile enough for this as well.
- Background color: Light gray (#F6F6F8) for a clean, modern look in the light theme and very dark gray (#101622) in the dark theme.
- Accent color: A vibrant analogous purple (#7D65E8) for interactive elements and highlights.
- Headlines: 'Space Grotesk', a modern sans-serif font, suitable for headlines, maximum readability with techy touch.
- Body: 'Inter' to ensure excellent readability across the diverse user base.
- Consistent use of outlined Material Symbols for a modern, accessible design.
- Modular design, aligned with award-winning portfolio pieces.
